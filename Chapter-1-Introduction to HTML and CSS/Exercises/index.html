Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a rich and creamy North Indian curry made with marinated grilled chicken simmered in a velvety tomato-based sauce. It's mildly spiced, slightly sweet, and packed with flavor — best served with naan or rice!

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Method

  1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Grill or sauté the marinated chicken until cooked and slightly charred. Set aside.
  3. In a pan, heat butter and sauté the chopped onions until golden brown.
  4. Add the tomato puree and cook until the oil starts to separate.
  5. Stir in the cooked chicken and mix well with the sauce.
  6. Pour in the cream and sprinkle garam masala. Let it simmer for 5–7 minutes on low heat.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
