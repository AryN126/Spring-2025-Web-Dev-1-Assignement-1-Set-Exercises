Manjummel Boys

Plot Overview

"Manjummel Boys" is a gripping Malayalam survival thriller based on a true incident involving a group of friends from the town of Manjummel who go on a trip to Kodaikanal. Their adventurous outing takes a life-threatening turn when one of them falls into the notorious Guna Caves, and the others must rally together in a desperate rescue mission.
